## SLIDE 2 — THE PROBLEM

**Raise your hand if you've ever wanted to make something with your hands — and then just... didn't.**

Not because you lacked materials.
Not because you lacked time.

Because you didn't know *what* to make.

That blank moment is where most creativity dies.

---

## SLIDE 3 — THE INSIGHT

**The missing piece isn't a tutorial.
It's a reason.**

People don't need another Pinterest board.
They need something that connects *what they feel* to *what they make*.

And then — they need a reason to keep it.

---

## SLIDE 4 — WHAT IS MOODMUSE

**MoodMuse is a magical museum that grows every time you feel something.**

It transforms your mood and whatever is within arm's reach into a meaningful creative project — then preserves what you made as a collectible artifact in your own personal museum.

Not a craft app.
Not an art generator.
A museum of *you*.

---

## SLIDE 5 — HOW IT WORKS

**Three steps. One feeling. One artifact.**

**1. Name the feeling.**
Ten moods. Pick the one that's loudest today.

**2. Show what's nearby.**
Canvas, markers, pencils — whatever's on your desk. AI sees it.

**3. Receive your project.**
A specific, emotionally meaningful creation designed for your mood and your materials. Under 30 minutes. No generic crafts.

---

## SLIDE 6 — THE MAGIC

**Before you've even started — it already has a name.**

Every project comes with a museum identity:

- An artifact name: *"The Stars She Took With Her"*
- A museum room: *Hall of Nostalgia*
- A curator's description
- A meaning — what this object preserves

Your craft isn't just a craft.
It's a museum piece before it exists.

---

## SLIDE 7 — MAKE IT

**You create it. With your hands. In under 30 minutes.**

Step-by-step guidance:
1. Draw yourself in the center of the canvas
2. Surround yourself with symbols of recent memories
3. Connect them like constellations
4. Add color for warmth, pain, excitement, possibility
5. Title it at the bottom

This isn't following a template.
This is making something that means something.

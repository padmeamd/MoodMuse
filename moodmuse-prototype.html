✦ The Alchemist's Plan ✦

A Constellation in the Palm

A small shell map of the people and places you carry — strung so it can hang where light finds it.

Why this, for nostalgia

Nostalgia is the feeling of carrying somewhere you can no longer return to. This makes that map holdable — a constellation you can keep in one hand.

From your cabinet

3 shells · blue satin ribbon · white cardstock · gold marker

The making
  1. Lay the shells out as a small constellation — each one a person or place.
  2. Mark a tiny gold star on every shell with the marker.
  3. Cut a strip of cardstock; write one line of where each star leads.
  4. Thread the ribbon through, knotting at each shell so the map holds its shape.
